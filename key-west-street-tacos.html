Key West Street Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb white fish (snapper or cod)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • Small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ½ cup diced mango

Instructions:

  1. Season fish with cumin, chili powder, and lime juice.
  2. Sauté or grill until flaky.
  3. Warm tortillas and top with fish, cabbage, avocado, and mango.
