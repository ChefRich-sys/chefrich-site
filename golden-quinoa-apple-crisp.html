Golden Quinoa Apple Crisp

Ingredients:

  • 2 large apples, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup cooked quinoa
  • ¼ cup rolled oats
  • 2 Tbsp almond flour
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Toss apples with lemon juice and cinnamon. Place in a small baking dish.
  2. Mix quinoa, oats, almond flour, and maple syrup for the topping.
  3. Spread over apples and bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.
