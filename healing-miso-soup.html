Healing Miso Soup

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp mellow white miso
  • 1 cup spinach
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ cup kelp noodles
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a light simmer, add mushrooms and ginger.
  2. Add spinach and kelp noodles, cook 3 minutes.
  3. Turn off heat. Stir miso into a small bowl of hot broth, then add back.
  4. Drizzle with sesame oil before serving if desired.
