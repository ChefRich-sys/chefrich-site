Autumn Ember Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb carrots, chopped
  • 1 sweet potato, diced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 cups ChefRich vegetable stock
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. Roast carrots, sweet potato, and onion at 400Â°F for 25 minutes.
  2. Blend with garlic, paprika, and vegetable stock until smooth.
  3. Simmer for 10 mins, season to taste, and finish with lime juice.
