🍲 ChefRich Autumn Ember Soup

Roasted carrots, sweet potatoes, and smoky paprika blended into a vibrant, warming soup. Finished with lime juice to brighten the flavor. A signature bowl of nourishment.

🏷️ Vegan • Gluten-Free • Anti-Inflammatory • Immune Supportive • ChefRich Signature Dish • Holiday Table

🍽️ Yield:

4–6 servings

⏱️ Prep Time:

10 minutes

⏱️ Cook Time:

35 minutes

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 lb carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 small yellow onion, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, unpeeled
  • 1½ Tbsp avocado oil (or brush with ChefRich vegetable stock for oil-free)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 4 cups ChefRich vegetable stock
  • Juice of 1 lime

🔪 Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Place carrots, sweet potato, onion, and garlic on a lined baking sheet.
  2. Drizzle with avocado oil or brush with stock. Roast for 25–30 minutes until golden and tender.
  3. Remove garlic skins and place all vegetables in a high-speed blender with paprika, cumin, and 3 cups of vegetable stock.
  4. Blend until smooth and velvety. Add remaining cup of stock to adjust thickness.
  5. Transfer to a saucepan, warm gently, and stir in lime juice just before serving.

🌿 Garnish Suggestions:

  • Toasted pumpkin seeds
  • Coconut yogurt or swirl of organic cream
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley

🌀 Variations:

  • Spicy Ember: Add a pinch of cayenne or harissa paste
  • Golden Root Version: Add ½ tsp turmeric and a parsnip to the roasting mix
