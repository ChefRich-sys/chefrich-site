🍵 ChefRich Healing Miso Soup

A light, fast, and soothing miso soup with spinach, mushrooms, kelp noodles, and fresh ginger. Immune-boosting and deeply nourishing.

🏷️ Immune Supportive • Anti-Inflammatory • Vegan • Gluten-Free • Make It Fast

🍽️ Yield:

2–3 servings

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 Tbsp mellow white miso
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • ½ cup sliced mushrooms
  • ½ cup kelp noodles or rice noodles
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)

🔪 Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a gentle simmer. Add mushrooms and ginger and cook 5 minutes.
  2. Add spinach and noodles; cook 2–3 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, dissolve miso in a bit of hot broth, then stir into pot off heat.
  4. Finish with a drizzle of sesame oil if desired. Serve warm.
