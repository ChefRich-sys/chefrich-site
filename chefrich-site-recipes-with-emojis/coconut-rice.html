🥥 ChefRich Coconut Rice

Creamy coconut milk and aromatic rice blended with toasted shredded coconut. A tropical, satisfying side dish.

🏷️ Vegan • Gluten-Free • Naturally Sweetened • Smart Carbs • World Flavors

🍽️ Yield:

4–6 servings

⏱️ Prep Time:

5 minutes

⏱️ Cook Time:

40 minutes

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1½ cups jasmine, basmati, or brown rice
  • 2 cups full-fat organic coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • 2½ Tbsp unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 Tbsp organic coconut oil (or avocado oil)

🔪 Instructions:

  1. Rub oil on the bottom of a medium pot with tight lid.
  2. Add rice, coconut milk, water, salt, and shredded coconut. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to a low simmer, cover, and cook until rice is tender (35–40 minutes).
  4. Remove from heat and let sit covered for 5 minutes. Fluff and serve.
