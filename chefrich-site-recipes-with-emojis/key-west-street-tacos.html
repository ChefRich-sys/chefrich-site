🌮 ChefRich Key West Street Tacos

Seasoned white fish wrapped in warm corn tortillas with avocado and mango salsa. A Key West favorite made healthy.

🏷️ Seafood Favorites • World Flavors • Diabetes-Friendly • High Protein • Heart Health

🍽️ Yield:

4 servings

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 lb white fish (snapper, mahi mahi, or cod)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • Small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • ½ cup diced mango

🔪 Instructions:

  1. Season fish with cumin, chili powder, and lime juice. Let sit 10 minutes.
  2. Grill or sauté fish until flaky. Warm tortillas.
  3. Assemble tacos with fish, cabbage, avocado, and mango salsa. Serve with lime wedges.
