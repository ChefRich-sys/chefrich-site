🍎 ChefRich Golden Quinoa Apple Crisp

A naturally sweet, gluten-free crisp made with apples and a golden topping of quinoa, oats, and maple.

🏷️ Gluten-Free • Naturally Sweetened • Desserts • Smart Snacks & Sweets

🍽️ Yield:

4 servings

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 2 large apples, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup cooked quinoa
  • ¼ cup rolled oats
  • 2 Tbsp almond flour
  • 2 Tbsp maple syrup

🔪 Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Toss apple slices with lemon juice and cinnamon. Place in baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, combine quinoa, oats, almond flour, and maple syrup. Crumble over apples.
  3. Bake for 25–30 minutes until top is golden and apples are tender.
